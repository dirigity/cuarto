We are students from a high school of spain, and we are really worried about the situation that refugees are suffering nowadays. The bottom line of this project is to aware the most people possible about the challenging conditions that some people are being forced to go through to ensure a better future. People think that refugees come only because of wars but there are other causes that motivate people to risk their lives to emigrate like for example african people want to escape from the epidemics that destroy their countries. But when refugees get to europe they hit a death end because our society isn’t ready to admit that much people. The only solution that could be accomplished in a near future is to make the country they belong a safer place. This is the purpose of our campaign is all about. We want to show people that the right way of doing things is to help before they need it, so they don’t have to get to a situation of need.
